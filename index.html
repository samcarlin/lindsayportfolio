Lindsay Oliver

Lindsay Oliver is an Emmy-nominated Anchor and Reporter at Spectrum News 1 Ohio, a statewide 24/7 cable news channel. It is the power-on station for Spectrum's 1.6 million cable subscribers in Ohio and is available to the state's more than 2 million digital subscribers. She is based in Columbus, but covers stories all over the Buckeye State, including in her home community of Cleveland, and she's provided network coverage for Spectrum stations all over the nation.
